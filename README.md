# Clinic Management & Appointment Booking Platform 🏥

A streamlined, user-centric healthcare web application designed to simplify medical scheduling and patient management. This platform bridges the gap between patients and healthcare providers, offering intuitive appointment scheduling, automated reminders, and a secure dashboard for both doctors and patients.

🔗 **[Live Demo URL](https://clinic-with-appointment-scheduling.vercel.app/)**

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **Smart Appointment Scheduling:** Interactive booking system allowing patients to filter by specialty, select their preferred doctor, and view real-time open time slots.
* **Patient & Doctor Dashboards:** Dedicated views for patients to track their medical history/upcoming visits, and for medical staff to manage daily schedules and patient queues.
* **Automated Status Notifications:** Real-time updates and reminders for booking confirmations, cancellations, and schedule adjustments.
* **Prescription & Records Hub:** Secure portal for doctors to upload digital prescriptions and clinical notes, accessible instantly by the patient.
* **Dynamic Availability Engine:** Automated calendar management that prevents double-booking and allows doctors to customize their working hours and break intervals.
